# Komikku
## A free and open source manga reader which is based off TachiyomiSY & Mihon/Tachiyomi. This fork is meant to provide new & useful features while regularly take features/updates from Mihon or other forks like SY, J2K and Neko...
### This guide will take you through the whole setup process for the app, which will include:
- Installing the App
- Adding the extension repo to be able to access Manga websites
- Setting up tracking functionality so you can keep your progress across different websites
- Sync functionality for easy device switching
